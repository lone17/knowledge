#maths/statistics 

 A probability density function $f(x, \theta)$ 
 - when viewed as a function of $x$ with $\theta$ fixed (i.e. $f(x | \theta)$ -> **probability density function**
 - when viewed as a function of $\theta$ with $x$ fixed (i.e. $f(\theta | x)$ -> **[[likelihood function]]**
